Screen, treat and track anxiety and depression with STAND

Feeling stressed?

Find out if you are experiencing symptoms of anxiety or depression with the Mental Health Tracker, a 5-10 minute survey offered through the Screening and Treatment for Anxiety and Depression (STAND) program.

  Use the Mental Health Tracker  

With STAND, students can screen, track and treat their anxiety and depression. After completing the Mental Health Tracker, you can view your personalized feedback and immediately enroll in a STAND service that best fits your needs. You can also come back and use the tracker as often as every two weeks.

By participating in STAND you are contributing to a large UCLA research initiative on depression, the Depression Grand Challenge. All Depression Grand Challenge studies are aimed at cutting the global burden of depression in half by 2050. Your information is kept confidential and will only be used for research purposes after you provide us with your informed consent.

Participation in the Mental Health Tracker and all STAND studies is voluntary. To learn more, please visit our website.

Please, email STAND@mednet.ucla.edu with any questions about STAND and the Mental Health Tracker.
