STAND Mental Health Tracker

Thank you for your interest in STAND! We noticed that you created an account but did not complete the Mental Health Tracker. The Mental Health Tracker is a 5-10 minute symptom survey, which will allow you to see your scores and register for additional services within STAND. With STAND, students can screen, treat and track their anxiety and depression symptoms. (Learn more about STAND and the Mental Health Tracker.)

To take the Mental Health Tracker:

  • Sign in to your account
  • Complete the registration steps, if you haven't done so
  • Complete the Mental Health Tracker

By participating in STAND you are contributing to a large research initiative on depression, the UCLA Depression Grand Challenge. The Mental Health Tracker is part of a STAND research study. All STAND studies are aimed at cutting the global burden of depression in half by 2050. Your information is kept confidential and will only be used for research purposes after you provide us with your informed consent.

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us at STAND@mednet.ucla.edu.

Thank you!
